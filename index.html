About Me

I'm a professional guitarist with over 15 years of experience performing and teaching. My journey with the guitar began when I was just 12 years old, and since then, music has become my life's passion.

I've performed in venues across the country and collaborated with various artists in the music industry. My teaching approach focuses on helping students discover their unique musical voice while building a solid technical foundation.

Whether you're a beginner looking to play your first chords or an advanced player seeking to refine your skills, I'm here to guide you on your musical journey.
